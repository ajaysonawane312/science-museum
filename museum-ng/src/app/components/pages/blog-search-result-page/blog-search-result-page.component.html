Catalysis

For the first time in the country, the laboratory established the triangular link between catalyst development, catalyst manufacturing and catalyst users. Commercialization of the catalyst for xylene isomerization. A unique process for the catalytic vapor phase oxidation of Toluene to Benzaldehyde
  • Catalysis Pilot Plant at CSIR-NCL which manufactured a few 100 kilograms of catalysts for commercial testing and production
  • 1990

Patenting & Global Technology Transfer

CSIR-NCL filed several patents in the US and were granted. This attracted the attention of the major global Industries who came to the laboratory looking for research partnerships. The Laboratory forayed into the area of polycarbonate chemistry and secured several seminal patents in the area, which attracted GE, the world's largest producer of polycarbonates to the laboratory resulting in the longest sustained industrial collaboration in CSIR.
  • Commercial Plant for production of Vinyl Esters at MEHK Chemicals Pvt. Ltd., Thane, Maharashtra
  • 2000

Innovation, Entrepreneurship & Partnership Models

CSIR-NCL Floated a company called "Entrepreneurship Development Center" to manage a technology business incubator. Proprietary processes for the manufacturing of THPE, ATBS, Epichlorohydrin, and chiral active pharmaceutical intermediates. Expertise in polymer science resulted in development of technologies for water purification using nanofiltration, fuel cell membranes, and biomedical polymers for ocular implants.
  • Vinati Organics ATBS Plant, Maharashtra
