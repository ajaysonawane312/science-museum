Shanti Swarup Bhatnagar Awardees

To honor Dr. Shanti Swarup Bhatnagar, CSIR established the Shanti Swarup Bhatnagar Award for Science and Technology in 1957. This Award recognizes outstanding contributions across various research disciplines. Now known as the Vigyan Yuva: Shanti Swarup Bhatnagar (VY-SSB) Award (Since 2024), it continues to be one of the highest honors for scientists in India.
