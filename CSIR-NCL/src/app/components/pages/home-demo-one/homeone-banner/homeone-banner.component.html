Shri. Jawaharlal Neharu Open Laboratory

The history of National Chemical Laboratory dates back to 6th April 1947. On this day, the foundation stone for NCL was laid. On 3rd January 1950, this laboratory was declared open by the then first Prime Minister of India, Pandit Jawaharlal Nehru.
