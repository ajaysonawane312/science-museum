Padma Awards

The Padma Awards are among the highest civilian honors in India, announced annually on the eve of Republic Day. They are awarded in three categories: Padma Vibhushan (for exceptional and distinguished service), Padma Bhushan (for distinguished service of a higher order), and Padma Shri (for distinguished service). These awards aim to recognize achievements across various fields and disciplines that involve a significant element of public service.
